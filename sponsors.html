A Note From the Board

Scope is a USC computer science club founded on one main idea: to build and learn cool things with great people. We are working to create a community of bright computer science students that want to learn and prepare themselves for careers as engineers.

We believe in inclusivity and openness. That's why all projects that students build are open-source and hosted on public GitHub repositories.

Scope offers a direct path to getting in touch with bright and motivated computer science students at USC. We offer four sponsorship tiers that allow your organization a variety of ways to get involved annually. These plans are flexible. Please get in touch if you would like more information or to discuss specific ways we can help your organization.

Check out the plans below and contact us by email or at 213-444-9020.

We truly appreciate any and all support from you.
